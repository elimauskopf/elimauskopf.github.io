Eli Mauskopf is an experienced software engineer based in Philadelphia, PA and graduated from Bucknell University with a B.S. in Computer Science in 2020. While working in web development after graduating was sustainable and surely a knowledge-boosting experience, he longed for the same enjoyment that he got from gaming after a long day of work, and felt a need to learn the tools necessary to start making his own experiences. A lifelong gamer and lover of difficult, substantive, and humorous games, Eli has managed to put together a few short projects that are fun, challenging, and fit the mold of his style and vision. Over the past year, Eli has been developing Slaughter Void, his largest project yet, which he hopes to release in early 2026.
