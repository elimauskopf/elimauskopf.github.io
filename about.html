Eli Mauskopf is a young and experienced software engineer from Philadelphia, PA and graduated from Bucknell University with a B.S. in Computer Science in 2020. While working in web development after graduating was sustainable and surely a knowledge-boosting experience, he longed for the same enjoyment that he got from gaming after a long day of work, and felt a need to learn the tools necessary to start making his own experiences. A lifelong gamer and lover of difficult, substantive, and humorous games, Eli has managed to put together a few short projects that are fun, challenging, and fit the mold of his style and vision. Over the past year, Eli has put his career on hold to grasp the skills and tools necessary to make compelling and fun games. He has absorbed and applied a lot of new knowledge, and while still learning every day would also like to extend his services to an excellent game studio when the time is right.
